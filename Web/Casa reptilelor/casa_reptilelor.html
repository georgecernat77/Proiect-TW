Reptile House

Description

From chameleons to snakes, from turtles to crocodiles and from the famous tarantulas

to centipedes, the House of Reptiles hosts both cold-blooded animals and arthropods feared even by

experts.

If you want to see these animals, visit the Reptile House of our Zoo,

and discover the secrets of invertebrates and reptiles brought here from all corners of the world!
