Hall of the Deapths

What is the Hall of The Deapths?

It's an underground aquarium, hosting over 200 species of aquatic animals.

Here guests can interact and admire Atlantic bottlenose dolphins, exotic fish, turtles and even more. The 400,000-gallon, 12-foot-deep saltwater pool offers two main viewing points. The dolphins in the aquarium are able for release back into the wild.
