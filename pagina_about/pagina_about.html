About Us

Who Are We?

Zoo Web Manager is the perfect place where you can find animals that you've never seen before and create new experiences. Whether you choose to spend some time by visting our main attractions, including Reptile House, Halls of the Depths and the Adventure Park, or visit the animals (almost 200 different spieces), you will never get bored. Our zoo garden is a special place where visitors can experience the beauty of nature.

On our website, you will find informations about animals in zoos, including interesting facts, habits, and details about our unique attractions. Most importantly, you can expand your knowledge by browsing through our vast selection of species using our dedicated filters and see the impressive details about each animal.
